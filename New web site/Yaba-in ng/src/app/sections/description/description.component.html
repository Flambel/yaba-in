Yaba-Innovation, Who are we?

Yaba-In is a limited liability company initiated in 2018 by yong Cameroonian engineers serving Africa and the whole world through the development of IT and innovative solutions. We are a digital transformation consultancy and software development company that provides cutting edge engineering solutions, helping companies and enterprise clients untangle complex issues that always emerge during their digital evolution journey. Our teams are mainly composed of IT specialists who are competent, disciplined and passionate.
